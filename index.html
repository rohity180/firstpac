A LITTLE ABOUT US

First Packers and Movers are one of the leading packers and movers company based in Mumbai that provide reliable and timely services in the field of packing and moving.Over the years we have built up a reputation that has inspired our customers to come back to us time and again. We have new customers almost every day and we make all efforts to ensure that more and more people know about us and the quality of services we provide.

Being a famous packers and movers, our services in packing and moving goods is outstanding. We offer a number of services like Unpacking Service, Value Added Services, Local Shifting Service, Corporate Goods Relocation, Household Goods Warehousing, Relocation Services, Car Carrier Services, Packaging Service etc. We adhere to all the National and international standards while packing and relocating your goods- both in India and abroad. Our clients include not just individuals but also numerous corporates. We, at First Packers and Movers, also provide hassle-free transport of your vehicles, such as cars, scooters and bikes without causing any damage. We, in fact are very specialized in 2-wheeler caring.

As like any other company, Customers are our primary concern and their contentment with our services means a lot to us. We understand that we exist because our customers have faith in us. Therefore we constantly endeavor to maintain their trust in us.
