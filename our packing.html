Packaging materials used by us

Our quality of service can be easily predicted by looking at the packaging technique and packaging materials used by us

Cardboard boxes

These are Light-weight and durable. after packing the maerials, they are kept inside these boxes, and are securely sealed by us for an easy and secure relocations.

Bubble Wraps

bubble wraps are a must if the items which need to be transported are fragile or delicate, so as to ensure a secure relocation. they help keeping the fragile items safe.

Wooden crates

items which may get easily deformed during the relocation, such as clothings, mattresses, or other plastck soft items are placed inside wooden crates after the packing.

Plastic stretch wraps

Each and Every stuff is sealed with atleast a few layes of stretch wrap. so that it becomes easy to carry, and the wraps also protect the items from moisture, humidity.

Poly strapping tools

these are the tools which help in strapping the containers together. So, nothing moves on it's own during the transit, and it provides an extra seal and more security to items.

Labels

We exclusively use labels such as fragile, delicate, books, this side up. It makes easily categorize the items and help us to focus more on securing the delicate items.
